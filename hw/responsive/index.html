After placing your order, your package should be shipped out within 24-72 hours after your order is placed, and you will receive an email with the tracking number soon after. USPS is our primary carrier for U.S customers, so depending upon where you live, packages are usually delivered within 4-7 days from the date of shipment. However, this shipping time is subject to change. PLEASE NOTE: Due to high order volume in recent weeks, shipping rates may be slightly slower than normal. We apologize for any inconveniences that this may cause.

If your order has been processing for more than 72 hours, please contact us at officialgriptec@gmail.com and we'll help resolve the issue immediately. PLEASE NOTE: Due to high order volume in recent weeks, shipping rates may be slightly slower than normal. We apologize for any inconveniences that this may cause.

CAN I TRACK MY ORDERS? When we dispatch your order from our warehouse we will send you tracking information for your order via email. If you have any concerns, please contact us at officialgriptec@gmail.com

DO YOU ACCEPT RETURNS? Our refund policy lasts 30 days. If 30 days have gone by since your purchase, unfortunately, we can’t offer you a refund or exchange. Perishable goods such as food, flowers, newspapers or magazines cannot be returned. We also do not accept products that are intimate or sanitary goods, hazardous materials, or flammable liquids or gases. There is often some processing time before a refund is officially posted. If you’ve done all of this and you still have not received your refund yet, please contact us at officialgriptec@gmail.com and we will have the issue resolved as quickly as we possibly can.
